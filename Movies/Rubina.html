Rubina: Pathaan: Male Gaze
We only see her standing behind the villain as he delivers his big monologue. Deepika’s role may appear to be limited to being a “bait” used by the villain to lure in his prey at first glance. She is there to sing and dance for her male counterparts while they do all the important work and carry on the story. And she will eventually find herself in the arms of her hero.
