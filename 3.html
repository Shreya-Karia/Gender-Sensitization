Male and Female Gaze
Male Gaze: From a masculine, heterosexual perspective that presents and represents women as sexual objects for the pleasure of the heterosexual male viewer. (Eaton, 2008)
Female Gaze: “There is a focus on how female directors have expressed themselves as people who identify as female. Resonant themes in women’s documentaries are characterised as tendencies, including the telling of stories from female points of view and an interest in issues that particularly impact women.” (French, 2021)
